01 — about_me

Who I Am

Engineer. Problem solver. Lifelong learner.

Hi, I'm Surya — an Android developer who started his journey as an intern at Philips and earned a full-time role through consistent performance and a passion for building things that matter. Over the past 3 years, I've been engineering Android applications that ship to millions of users across the globe.

My day-to-day work spans writing clean Kotlin, building declarative UIs with Jetpack Compose, and architecting shared business logic through KMM (Kotlin Multiplatform Mobile) — so the same code runs on both Android and iOS. I also work closely with IoT integrations, connecting physical devices via Bluetooth to deliver real-time guidance inside apps.

Academically, I hold a Dual Degree — a combined B.Tech + M.Tech in Software Engineering from VIT University, which gave me a strong theoretical foundation to complement my hands-on industry experience.

I'm always looking forward — eager to explore new architectures, sharpen my craft, and grow into larger technical responsibilities. Every line of code I write is a step toward building something extraordinary.
